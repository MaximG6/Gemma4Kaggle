Download real font files before building:

Inter (OFL license):
  https://fonts.google.com/specimen/Inter
  Files needed: Inter-Regular.ttf, Inter-Medium.ttf, Inter-SemiBold.ttf, Inter-Bold.ttf
  Place in: assets/fonts/Inter/

JetBrains Mono (OFL license):
  https://www.jetbrains.com/lp/mono/
  Files needed: JetBrainsMono-Regular.ttf
  Place in: assets/fonts/JetBrainsMono/

The placeholder .ttf files in this directory are stubs and will cause
a render error. Replace them with the real files from the links above.
